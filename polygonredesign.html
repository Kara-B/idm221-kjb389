Polygon Redesign: A Brief Summary

Polygon.com is a website dedicated to bringing the latest news in gaming, entertainment, and other pop-culture to people online. As a website, I have referenced it many times when looking for news on upcoming games, but I found myself frustrated from the navigation on the site. With this in mind, I decided to consider what a redesign of the website might look like.

During this process, I :

  • Conducted heuristic studies on users of the website Polygon.com
  • Constructed low and high fidelity wireframes using tools like Figma
  • Redesigned the homepage into organized news categories
  • Redesigned the news article appearance into a layout that is both pleasing to the eye and functional for the user
