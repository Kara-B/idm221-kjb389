ArtNow: A Brief Summary

Me and a small team came up with an idea for a pop-up gallery buisness based in philly. Our mission was to create an experience that brings art and interactive experiences to people in many areas of philidelphia and eventually, other cities like Boston.

In our development, we :

  • Researched Competition
  • Performed SWOT analysis
  • Developed a thorough business plan
  • Identified opportunities for audience retention
  • Designed a budget
  • Considered buisness branding and identity
